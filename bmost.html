About the District

Greenfield District was established in 1892 and has grown into a harmonious blend of rural charm and modern amenities. Known for our annual harvest festival, lush parks, and strong community bonds.

Natural Beauty

Over 12 parks, 30+ miles of trails, and the serene Willow Lake — a haven for nature lovers.

Education First

Top-rated schools, a public library, and innovation hubs for lifelong learning.

Community Spirit

Volunteer programs, farmers markets, and cultural festivals unite residents year-round.
